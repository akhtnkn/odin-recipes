Potato Salad

Description

A traditional creamy potato salad, with eggs, celery and relish.

Ingredients

  • 5 potatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon celery salt
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

    ]
  1. Bring large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes abd cook for around 15 minutes until the potatoes are tender but still firm.
  2. Drain, cool, peel, and chop potatoes.
  3. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil; cover, remove from heat and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.
  4. Combine potatoes, eggs, celery, onion, relish, mayonnaise, mustard, garlic salt, celery salt, and pepper in large bowl. Mix together well and refrigerate until chilled.
