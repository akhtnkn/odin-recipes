Banana Bread

Description

A banana bread recipe with loads of banana flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Lightly grease 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Beat brown sugar and butter with electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
  4. Stir bana mixture into flour mixture until just combined and pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 60 minutes. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes and out onto wire rack to cool completely.
